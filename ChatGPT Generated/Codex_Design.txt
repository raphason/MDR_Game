
Codex Design
------------
- Permanent run-spanning archive.
- Entries include:
    1. Discovered Temper Rules
    2. Tool & Voucher effects
    3. Music Dance modifiers
    4. Dialogue outcomes and CI effects
    5. Refiners: stats, quotes, endings
    6. Lore entries (corporate history, Founder's quotes)
    7. Endings unlocked (Promotion, Reintegration, Secret Paths)

- Categories are searchable.
- Certain Contraband can "unlock" hidden Codex pages.
- Some Codex lore may only be readable with low CI or high Autonomy.
